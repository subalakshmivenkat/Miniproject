Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras

IIT Madras - Overview

Location: IIT P.O., Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Description: IIT Madras is one of India's most prestigious institutes and a global leader in education and research in various fields of science and technology. Founded in 1959, it offers cutting-edge undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, science, and technology. IIT Madras is known for its strong academic reputation, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and diverse research opportunities.

Notable Features:

  • World-class faculty and research labs
  • Strong industry connections and internships
  • Entrepreneurial ecosystem and startup incubation
  • Green campus with a focus on sustainability
