VIT University Chennai - Overview

Location: Vandalur-Kelambakkam Road, Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Description: VIT University Chennai, a part of the VIT group of institutions, is known for its academic excellence and innovative teaching methodologies. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, and management. The campus is equipped with modern infrastructure and provides a vibrant environment for learning and research.

Notable Features:

  • High placement record with top recruiters
  • Industry-aligned curriculum and collaborations
  • State-of-the-art labs and learning facilities
  • Active student clubs and cultural events
