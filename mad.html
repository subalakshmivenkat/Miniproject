Madras Christian College (MCC)

Madras Christian College - Overview

Location: Tambaram, Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Description: Founded in 1837, Madras Christian College (MCC) is one of the oldest and most prestigious liberal arts and sciences colleges in India. Known for its rich heritage, sprawling campus, and academic excellence, MCC offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs across various disciplines. The college has a strong focus on character building and holistic development.

Notable Features:

  • Historic campus with over 365 acres of greenery
  • Wide range of courses in arts, sciences, and commerce
  • Renowned alumni in various fields including arts, politics, and business
  • Active cultural and sports activities
